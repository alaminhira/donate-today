Our Donate Plan

With your donation you make an important contribution to their lives who are more in need. Let them to live again

Food for a family

During natural disaster we try to provide every family enough safe food and water (for drinking, cooking, bathing, etc.) So that they do not get sick by eating unhealthy food due to lack of safe food.

Shelter for a family

During natural disasters people become homeless. So we take initiative to build safe shelters and evacuate people as soon as possible.

Education for a child

In the aftermath of a natural disaster, we take steps to quickly engage students in their educational activities and reactivate educational institutions.
